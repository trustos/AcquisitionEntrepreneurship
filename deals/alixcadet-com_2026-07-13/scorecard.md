# Acquisition Scorecard — AlixCadet.com

**Verdict:** PASS — 35.2/100 (completeness 65%)

Cheap and cash-flowing, but a single-program AliExpress affiliate/SEO coupon site (the team's weakness), neglected and likely declining, ~100% revenue-concentrated, in a category under attribution assault (Honey/extension hijacking).

## Scores
- build_scale_fit: 1/5 (high) Affiliate/SEO/content business; growth = more content + more programs + audience work. Software skills add ~nothing (coupon-import automation is trivial and not the revenue driver).
- pmf_retention: 2/5 (low) Transient coupon-seekers, no loyalty; Telegram audience a mild plus; seller admits it's been neglected/likely declining.
- unit_economics: 2/5 (low) ~$245/mo net, 100% one affiliate program (AliExpress/Admitad), clawback-prone and seasonal (sale spikes), unverified; low ceiling for this team.
- moat_channel: 1/5 (high) Commodity coupon niche vs RetailMeNot/Honey/extensions; single SEO channel + single affiliate program; last-click attribution being hijacked industry-wide.
- growth_market: 2/5 (med) Claimed paths (Temu/SHEIN, display ads, Telegram) are all marketing work; category under structural pressure (Honey scandal, Google 2025 extension policy).
- tech_ip: 3/5 (med) WordPress, simple and transferable; content is coupon listings (low IP). Jetpack-only stats weaken verification.
- deal_quality: 3/5 (med) $4,900 BIN ~= 20x monthly / ~1.7x annual net = below-market/cheapish, but risk-appropriate; all-cash BIN, no seller financing implied.
- seller_risk: 1/5 (med) Neglected several months (likely declining), ~100% revenue concentration in one program, seasonal average, Jetpack-only stats, selling on Reddit + Flippa.

## Key unknowns
- Real monthly earnings trend (Admitad) vs the '~$300 average' — how much is 11.11/big-sale spikes and what are the last 3 months, given the neglect? Swings unit economics and seller-risk.
- Revenue concentration & transfer: 100% AliExpress/Admitad? Do the affiliate account and existing links transfer or must they be re-issued under a new account (often they don't transfer)?
- Telegram 6,000+ subs: organic vs bought, engagement, and whether channel ownership can actually transfer — the one potentially real off-Google asset.
- Traffic trend and quality (Search Console): any Google penalty/algo hit; how much US vs low-monetizing geos (Russia).
- Exposure to the affiliate-attribution shift (Honey/extension hijacking, Google 2025 extension policy) on AliExpress conversions.

## Seller questions
1. Screen-share the Admitad dashboard: 12-24 months of monthly earnings, confirmed vs pending/clawed-back. Is '~$300/mo' a trailing average, and what are the last 3 months? (unit economics + adverse selection; verifies revenue and decline)
2. How seasonal is it — what share of revenue is AliExpress big-sale periods (11.11, etc.) vs baseline months? (revenue quality)
3. Search Console + Jetpack: 12-month traffic trend, top pages/queries, any manual penalty or core-update drop, and AI Overview presence on coupon queries? (fragility)
4. Is revenue 100% AliExpress/Admitad? On sale, does the affiliate account transfer, or do I re-apply and re-issue every link (and does AliExpress permit it)? (concentration + transferability)
5. Telegram 6,000+ subs: how acquired (organic vs paid), current engagement/opens, any revenue today, and can channel ownership actually transfer to me? (asset verification)
6. Why sell at $4,900 now, how long has it been neglected, other bidders? Any trademark/policy risk using 'AliExpress' branding, or content that's AI-generated/duplicated? (adverse selection + legal)
