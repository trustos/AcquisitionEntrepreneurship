# Acquisition Scorecard — WebTracky

**Verdict:** PASS — 36.4/100 (completeness 72%)

Right shape (analytics SaaS in your wheelhouse, verified revenue, in budget) but hobby-tier and flat — ~$40 MRR from 5 subs after 7 months — in one of software's most saturated commodity markets (GA/Plausible/Fathom/PostHog/Umami), zero moat, priced at an expensive 6.3x, with no market interest (no offers in 5 months). Its only real distribution is the founder's YouTube, which doesn't transfer.

## Scores
- build_scale_fit: 3/5 (med) Analytics on Next/TS is squarely buildable/ownable by you — but that cuts both ways: it's a commodity you could rebuild in weeks, and the path to value is distribution/differentiation in a red ocean, not engineering.
- pmf_retention: 1/5 (high) 5 paying subs, ~$40 MRR, flat/erratic over 7 months (one $1,020 launch spike in Sep 2025 that fizzled). No proven PMF.
- unit_economics: 1/5 (med) $40 MRR at 95% margin but negligible and flat; analytics is storage/query-heavy, so margin may compress as event volume grows.
- moat_channel: 1/5 (high) Zero moat in a hyper-saturated market with free incumbents (GA, Plausible, Fathom, PostHog, Umami, Vercel/Cloudflare analytics). DR 0; the only channel is the founder's YouTube (TubeGuruji), which stays with him.
- growth_market: 1/5 (med) Huge but brutally commoditized market; a DR-0 entrant with 5 subs has no wedge. Revenue flat.
- tech_ip: 3/5 (med) Clean modern stack (Next/React/TS/Tailwind), transferable — but trivially rebuildable, so low IP value.
- deal_quality: 2/5 (med) $3,000 at 6.3x revenue for flat $40 MRR is expensive-for-the-traction; the market agrees — no offers in ~5 months. In budget, poor value.
- seller_risk: 3/5 (med) Founder is an identifiable coding YouTuber (TubeGuruji); verified revenue = low deception. The risk is 'nothing there' (a fizzled side-project launched to his audience), not fraud.

## Key unknowns
- Did the 5 customers come from the founder's YouTube, and does ANY of that distribution transfer — or does the only real asset walk away with him? (the crux)
- Why flat at ~$40 MRR for 7 months, and what was the Sep-2025 $1,020 spike (launch/lifetime deals)?
- Any differentiation vs free/cheap incumbents that would make someone pay for WebTracky specifically?
- Does 100% of code + the 5 accounts + domain transfer, and is the tracking script/infra self-contained?
- True margin as event volume scales (analytics = storage/query heavy).

## Seller questions
1. Of the 5 subscriptions: plan mix, churn, and why has revenue been flat ~$40/mo for 7 months (with the one $1,020 spike in Sep 2025)? (PMF/revenue)
2. Where did the paying customers come from — your YouTube (TubeGuruji)? Does any of that audience/distribution transfer, or stay with you? (channel — likely the only real, and non-transferable, asset)
3. What's the differentiation vs PostHog/Plausible/Fathom/GA/Umami, and why pay for WebTracky over free incumbents? (moat)
4. Why price at 6.3x revenue ($3,000 for ~$480/yr), and why no offers in ~5 months listed? (deal quality/adverse selection)
5. What transfers: 100% code ownership, domain, the 5 customer accounts, docs — and is the tracking script/infra self-contained? (IP/transfer)
6. Event-volume infra costs — is 95% margin durable as data scales? (unit economics)
