# Acquisition Scorecard — wattstovolts.com

**Verdict:** PASS — 33.6/100 (completeness 62%)

Well-built and fast-growing, but an SEO/content/ad business (the team's weakness) in the most AI-Overview-exposed niche, with unproven week-old AdSense revenue, sold at the peak of a 3-month ramp.

## Scores
- build_scale_fit: 1/5 (high) Calculators are ~10 lines of Watt's-law JS; growth is 100% SEO/content — the team's weakness. Software skills add almost nothing.
- pmf_retention: 2/5 (med) Real search demand but zero user loyalty; pure Google pass-through traffic.
- unit_economics: 2/5 (low) AdSense ~1 week old = no real revenue history; display-only, international/low-CPC, informational = low RPM.
- moat_channel: 1/5 (high) Single channel (Google organic), zero moat; commodity — Google's own answer box and rapidtables/inchcalculator rank.
- growth_market: 2/5 (med) Growing now but the category is structurally disintermediated by AI Overviews / instant answers.
- tech_ip: 3/5 (low) Simple and transferable, but trivially replicable; content provenance (AI-generated? 18 auto-translations?) unknown.
- deal_quality: 2/5 (unknown) No asking price provided.
- seller_risk: 1/5 (med) Brand-new site monetized last week, already for sale at the top of the ramp — classic build-to-flip; adverse selection.

## Key unknowns
- Actual AdSense revenue and RPM since approval (only days old) — swings unit economics; near-zero would confirm 'not a business yet'.
- Asking price and its basis — needed for deal quality, but does not change the PASS given the competence gate.
- Query/page mix and AI Overview presence on top terms — how much of the 459k impressions is being disintermediated; worst case confirms fragility.
- Content provenance and IP ownership (AI-generated? auto-translated?), and any prior Google penalties.
- Traffic composition: 100% Google organic vs any branded/direct.

## Seller questions
1. Screen-share the AdSense dashboard: actual daily earnings and page RPM since approval. (unit economics; also: why sell one week after monetizing?)
2. Why sell now, one week after monetizing? How many sites have you built and sold? (adverse selection)
3. Search Console by query and by page: share of clicks from the 'watts to volts' head term vs the blog; do top queries show an AI Overview? Trend through the last core update? (fragility)
4. Asking price and its basis — a multiple of what profit? (deal quality)
5. Is the content and are the 18 language versions AI-generated? Who owns the IP? Any manual penalties? What % of traffic is branded/direct vs Google organic? (IP + fragility)
6. What transfers — domain, GA/GSC, social handles, email? Note AdSense does not transfer; the new owner re-applies, so plan a day-one monetization gap. (transferability)
